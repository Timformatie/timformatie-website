# Security Policy

Security is core to what we do at Timformatie. We work in the Dutch healthcare
sector and hold **ISO/IEC 27001** and **NEN 7510** certification. We welcome
responsible disclosure of vulnerabilities in this website or our services.

## Reporting a vulnerability

Please email **[security@timformatie.nl](mailto:security@timformatie.nl)** with:

- a description of the issue and its potential impact;
- steps to reproduce (URLs, payloads, screenshots);
- any relevant configuration (browser, OS).

**Do not** open a public GitHub issue or pull request for security matters, and
please do not disclose the issue publicly until we have had a chance to address
it.

## What to expect

- **Acknowledgement** within 5 business days.
- A **status update** within 10 business days, including our assessment and an
  expected remediation timeline.
- Credit for your responsible disclosure, if you would like it.

## Scope

In scope:

- This repository and the websites it serves (e.g. `timformatie.nl`,
  `dev-website.timformatie.nl`).

Out of scope:

- Our data platform and product systems — for those, contact
  [security@timformatie.nl](mailto:security@timformatie.nl) and we will route
  your report appropriately.
- Findings that require physical access, social engineering, or denial-of-service
  testing.

## Safe harbor

We will not pursue legal action against researchers who act in good faith, follow
this policy, avoid privacy violations and service disruption, and give us
reasonable time to remediate before any disclosure.
